About
=====

Team
----
This work has been accomplished by a team of PhD students:

- Daniele Lunghi: Université Libre de Bruxelles, Belgium
- Gian Marco Paldino: Université Libre de Bruxelles, Belgium

Under the supervision of:

- Dr. Olivier Caelen: Worldline SA, Belgium
- Prof. Gianluca Bontempi: Université Libre de Bruxelles, Belgium

And it is partly based on the work of: 

- Dr. Yann-Aël Le Borgne: Université Libre de Bruxelles, Belgium

It was realised in the context of the paper `An Adversary model of fraudsters behaviour to improve oversampling in credit card fraud detection` currently under review.
